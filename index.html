How to use the simulator
🎯 Before the race: Drag the slider to preview what various points in the race look like — see projected finish times, on-track status, and expected arrival at each spectator spot.
🏃 During the race: The slider locks to live position. Drag forward to explore projections from the current position. You can't scrub backward — live data is king.
▶ Play mode: Hit play to watch an automatic simulation of the race unfold in real time (3x speed).
📍 Map sync: The simulator marker on the Live Map tab updates as you drag.
